A Journey of a Thousand Wings

It began in 2018 with a single hive in a backyard. Our founder, Arthur Sterling, realized that the local bee populations were dwindling rapidly. What started as a hobby became a passion, and eventually, a mission to connect people back to nature.

"Bees are the heartbeat of our food system," Arthur says. "Without them, the world loses its color and its sustenance. We created BeeHive Sponsorship to give everyone a chance to be part of the solution."

Arthur Sterling

Founder & Master Apiarist

"Our goal isn't just to produce honey, but to foster a community that understands and respects the intricate balance of our ecosystem."

Pure Harvest

Conservation

The Conservation Impact

Since our inception, we've successfully reintroduced 12 endangered bee species to the region. Our farm serves as a safe haven, free from the threats of industrial agriculture.

Why Support Local Beekeeping?

Supporting local hives means supporting your local environment. Local honey contains pollen from your area, which can help with seasonal allergies, and local apiaries contribute directly to the health of your neighborhood's gardens and parks.
