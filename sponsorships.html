Frequently Asked Questions

Can I visit my hive at any time?

Visits are scheduled to ensure bee safety and expert guidance. Sponsorships include a set number of guided visits, which can be booked via your dashboard.

What happens if the hive dies?

Nature can be unpredictable. If a colony fails due to natural causes, we will automatically transition your sponsorship to a healthy backup colony to ensure you still get your harvest share.

Do you ship the honey internationally?

Yes, we ship to most countries. Shipping costs for honey shares are calculated at checkout based on your location.
