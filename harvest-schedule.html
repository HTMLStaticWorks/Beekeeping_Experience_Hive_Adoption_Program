Collection Timeline

Extraction Day

The frames are pulled, uncapped, and spun in our industrial grade centrifugal extractors. This is the only day the honey is "raw" and "fresh from the comb".

Settling & Cold Filtering

We let the honey rest for 48 hours to let air bubbles escape. We use a coarse filter only to remove wax pieces, keeping all the beneficial pollen intact.

Sustainable Bottling

Our honey is bottled in recycled glass jars with cork lids, ready for labeling with your specific colony's name.

Preparing the Fields

Long before the harvest, we prepare the sanctuary. This includes planting over 50,000 seasonal wildflower seeds and ensuring the water sources are clean and accessible for the bees.
